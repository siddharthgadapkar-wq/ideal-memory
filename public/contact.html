FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and event planning process.

How far in advance should I book my event?

We recommend booking your event at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for weddings and large corporate events. However, we can accommodate shorter timelines depending on availability and event complexity.

Do you provide catering for all types of events?

Yes, we provide comprehensive catering services for all types of events including weddings, birthdays, corporate functions, and special occasions. Our menus can be customized to suit your preferences and dietary requirements.

What is included in your event planning packages?

Our packages include venue coordination, decoration, catering, entertainment setup, timeline management, and day-of coordination. Specific inclusions vary based on the package you choose.

Can you work within my budget?

Absolutely! We work with budgets of all sizes and will create a customized package that maximizes value within your specified budget range. We're transparent about costs and provide detailed quotes upfront.

Do you offer destination event planning?

Yes, we provide destination event planning services. While our primary service area is local, we can coordinate with vendors in other locations and provide remote planning assistance for destination events.

What if I need to make changes to my event?

We understand that plans can change. We're flexible and will work with you to accommodate changes when possible. We have a clear change policy that outlines timelines and any additional costs.
